How it works

Planting details

The app remembers everything you've planted  and where you planted it. It gives you suggestions on plant spacing  and suitable companion plants each time you add a new plant.

Severe weather

When the weather turns to custard , you'll be warned so you can take action to protect your fragile plants. You'll also be notified of things like overnight frost  warnings.

Irrigation

The app uses local climate data  to determine water requirements for different plants and alerts you when you need to water  your plants.

Sharing the harvest

When harvest time rolls around you get reminders when each crop reaches maturity , and the option to trade produce  with other users!
