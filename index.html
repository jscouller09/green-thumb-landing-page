How it works

Planting details

This app remembers everything you've planted and where. It gives you suggestions on plant spacing and suitable companion plants each time you add a new plant.

Severe weather

When the weather turns to custard you'll beb warned so you can take action to protect your fragile plants.

Irrigation

The app uses local data to determine plant water requirements and alerts you when you need to water.

Sharing the harvest

When harvest time rolls around you get reminders for all your crops, and the option to trade produce with other users!
